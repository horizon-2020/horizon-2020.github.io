OPEN AIR

Open Air: Katie Noonan + Louise King

Celebrating place, Open Air is a monthly concert series, streamed from the Sunshine Coast’s most spectacular locations, featuring some of the region’s most celebrated artists.

Next up in the series is a powerhouse duo; five-time ARIA award-winning artist Katie Noonan and supremely talented classical musician Louise King of Cello Dreaming.

This beautiful classic contemporary concert features both solo and duo performances from these two incredible artists, filmed against a lovely sunset at Chambers Island; a stunning celebration of our Sunshine Coast riverways.

Don’t miss this special performance, available online here and via Facebook at 4pm on Sun 5 Jul. 

Credits
