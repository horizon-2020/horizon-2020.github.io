Meet the Eumundi School of Rock Rockers

Layla – Vocals – Age 11

What is your favourite thing about being a part of Eumundi School of Rock?
Favourite part of the School of Rock so far has been being mentored by talented and helpful teachers.

Who would you love to play on stage with?
I would love to sing with Katie Noonan on stage one day as she is such an amazing musician.

What is your absolute favourite rock song?
My favourite rock song is "Don't Stop Me Now" by Queen. 

 

Senti – Drums – Age 13

What is your favourite thing about being a part of Eumundi School of Rock?
Getting to hang out with new friends who love music as much as I do.

Who would you love to play on stage with?
I would love to sing with Guy Sebastian.

What is your absolute favourite rock song?
"Better" by Screaming Jets.

 

Jasper – Guitar -  Age 12

What is your favourite thing about being a part of Eumundi School of Rock?
The best thing about Eumundi School of Rock is getting to know new people who love music like me and at the end of the lessons we get to play in a concert with our new band.

Who would you love to play on stage with?
All the new people in my new band and if I ever had a chance, I would love to play with John Butler, Tom Morello, John Mayer and ACDC.

What is your absolute favourite rock song?
So many, but "Thunder Struck" and "Sultans of Swings" are probably the funnest to play.

 

Charlee Williams – Saxophone – Age 11

What is your favourite thing about being a part of Eumundi School of Rock?
Playing music with my friends.

Who would you love to play on stage with?
Zac Hurren.

What is your absolute favourite rock song?
"Boulevard of Broken Dreams"

 

Beau Butler- Keys - Age 12

What is your favourite thing about being a part of Eumundi School of Rock?
My Favourite thing about ESOR is being able to play with other people on stage.

Who would you love to play on stage with?
I would love to play with Katie Noonan on stage.

What is your absolute favourite rock song?
My favourite rock song is "Beds are Burning" by Midnight Oil.

 

Lacey – Bass - Age 12

What is your favourite thing about being a part of Eumundi School of Rock?
The fact that we get to be surrounded by the other kids with the same passion as you.

Who would you love to play on stage with?
I would love to play on stage with SIA, Amy Shark, Guy Sebastian and Tones and I.

What is your absolute favourite rock song?
My absolute favourite rock song is "One" by Metallica. My absolute favourite Aussie rock song is "Solid Rock" by Goanna.

 

Rafael Estrade- Singer- Age 9

What is your favourite thing about being a part of Eumundi School of Rock?
It’s lots and lots and lots and lots of fun.

Who would you love to play on stage with?
Guy Sebastian.

What is your absolute favourite rock song?
"Beds are Burning" by Midnight Oil.
