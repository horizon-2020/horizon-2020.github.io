field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"> <p><em>Field Trip</em> is a national online research symposium focused on creative practice at the intersection of art, science, technology and the environment.</p> <p>The symposium will traverse the continent exploring the ethics of interdisciplinary art and the role of creativity in reimagining the future.</p> <p>The event features some of Australia’s leading artists and includes keynote presentations, panels, interactive artworks and participatory experiences that explore the possibilities of presenting ecologically engaged creative work in virtual environments.</p> <p>To explore the full program and register to attend this free symposium, head over to the Arts Front website: <a
