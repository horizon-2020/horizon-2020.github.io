The Bunker Spoken Word Series 2020

The Bunker Spoken Grand Final returns in 2020 in our first live event since taking shelter in the online format!

Our 10 Spoken Word finalists have progressed from the heats and will compete in the grand final event at the Imperial Hotel, Eumundi. 

The Grand Final features stronger competition than ever before, with local poets performing live and interstate competitors projected live on screen.

Joined by the multi-award winning Hollie McNish as our feature poet beaming in from Scotland, we’re set to kick off our return to local venues in true Bunker style and can’t wait for you to join!

Bunker Spoken Word winner will be awarded the $500 cash prize courtesy of our longstanding partners, the Imperial Hotel Eumundi and University of the Sunshine Coast.

PARTNERS

The Bunker Spoken Word is supported by the Regional Arts Development Fund, a partnership between the Queensland Government and Sunshine Coast Council to support local arts and culture in regional Queensland.
